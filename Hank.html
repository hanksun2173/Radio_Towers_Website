Hank Sun

Programmer, Project Manager

Hank is what one might call a creative, in all sense of the word. Being a senior at Tufts pursuing a B.S. in both Cognitive and Brain Science and Computer Science, he sees technology and scientific research as a playground for his creative imagination. From coming up with out-of-the-box solutions in programming to his novel writing, Hank is constantly pushing the boundaries of what creativity means to him and passionately applying it to his work and studies.

His professional experience spans web development, mobile app development, human-computer interaction, and user analyses in the tech sphere, and beyond it he has also found himself in the realm of project management, market research, design, and much more. With a passion for communication, he has both conducted research in Psycholinguistics with the Tufts Psycholinguistics lab as well as served as a teaching assistant for the Programming Language course at Tufts.

In his spare time, Hank can be found working diligently on his novel, spending copious amounts of time and money in bookstores for his next favourite read, and occasionally playing the guitar. He is a people person and is always excited to make new friends and make new connections, eager to impart his creative spirit and his friendly attitude to those around him.
