In 1985, Hurricane Elena decimated Southern Florida.

As you climbed out of the wreckage of your home, all you could see are empty wastelands. With only a radio in your hand, you must find and fix the radio towers scattered among the barren landscape to find out what happened to your loved ones.

In Radio Towers,you play as a lone child in the aftermath of a Hurricane in Southern Florida. Stranded and alone, you must explore the desolate landscape to find and repair radio towers scattered throughout the area. As a player, you must help our protagonist climb each tower, avoiding obstacles and guide them through the trecherous climbs each tower presents. At the top, you must repair the broken wiring and attempt to hear through your radio any hints of where your loved ones might be.
Radio Towers is a 2D platformer with puzzle elements, where each tower presents a unique challenge to the player. The game focuses on themes of isolation, hope, and perseverance in the face of adversity. With its atmospheric setting and emotional narrative, Radio Towers aims to provide players with a memorable and engaging gaming experience.
